## When Reports Are Ready

- **All practices**: Reports are ready for use on the **3rd business day of the month** for the prior month's data.
- **Enterprise practices**: Your report is ready following the alert from Marketing Services. Please wait for their confirmation before generating your Enterprise MBR.

---

## Generating a Report

1. Go to the **Dashboard** (home page).
2. Type the practice name or Medspa ID in the search field.
   - You can also browse practices by segment tier (Momentum, Growth, Silver, Gold, Enterprise) using the filter buttons below.
3. Select the **Month** and **Year** for the reporting period.
4. Click **Generate Report**.
5. The tool will load data from Omni. This typically takes 30-60 seconds.
6. Once complete, you'll be taken to the **Editor** where you can review and edit the report.

---

## Editing a Report

In the Editor, the left sidebar contains editable sections:

- **Executive Summary** — AI-generated overview of the practice's month. You can edit this directly.
- **Assessments** — Strengths, opportunities, and areas to watch. Add, remove, or edit cards.
- **PSM Feedback** — Personalized coaching notes. Use `**text**` for bold formatting.
- **Key Metrics** — Net revenue, appointments, AOV, and more. All values are editable.
- **Performance Gauges** — Utilization, rebooking, retention targets. Editable.
- **Client Mix** — New vs. existing client counts.
- **Revenue Breakdown** — Service revenue, prepayment, retail, discounts, fees.
- **Memberships** — Active, new, cancelled, and MRR.
- **Marketing Performance** — Ad spend, leads, booked, completed, revenue. All editable.
- **Reviews** — Google and Facebook ratings.
- **Launches & Brand Bank** — Managed through the Monthly Assets tab (shared across all practices).

Changes auto-save as you type. Click the **Save** button to create a version checkpoint.

---

## Exporting a Report

- Click **Export PDF** to download the report as a PDF file.
- Click **Export PPTX** to download as a PowerPoint file.

The exported PDF matches what you see in the preview.

---

## Report Archive

Go to the **Report Archive** tab to find previously generated reports:

1. Search by practice name.
2. Click a practice to see all saved reports by month.
3. Click **Open** to reopen a report in the Editor.
4. Click **Versions** to see the version history and restore a previous save if needed.

---

## Monthly Assets

**Password**: `operations`

The Monthly Assets tab is where you upload Launches and Brand Bank content that gets included in every MBR for that month.

1. Select the month and year.
2. Upload a **Launches** file (PDF recommended to preserve hyperlinks).
3. Upload a **Brand Bank** image or PDF.
4. The tool uses AI to extract items from the uploads.
5. Click **Save Monthly Assets** to save.

These assets are shared — upload once and they appear in every practice's MBR for that month.

**Password**: `moxie2026`

## Important: Saving Your Work

After making any changes to an MBR, you **must click the Save button** to ensure your edits are saved. While the tool auto-saves drafts as you type, only clicking Save creates a permanent version that others can see and that will be preserved in the Report Archive.

---

## Supplies Savings

The MBR includes a high-level Supplies Savings summary for each practice. For a deeper dive into a practice's savings data — including detailed vendor breakdowns, transaction history, and rebate tracking — use the **Supplies Savings** tab in the navigation bar.

---

## Tips

- **Always click Save**: Auto-save keeps a working draft, but you must click Save to lock in your changes and create a restorable version.
- **Version History**: In the Editor sidebar, expand "Version History" to see and restore previous saves.
- **Segment Browser**: On the Dashboard, use the tier buttons to filter practices by Momentum, Growth, Silver, Gold, or Enterprise.
- **Marketing Section**: For non-Enterprise practices, only metrics are shown. Enterprise practices also see campaign-level breakdowns and AI recommendations.
